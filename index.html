#Environment

Environment Definition


" The environment refers to the natural surroundings or conditions in which living organisms exist. It encompasses the physical, chemical, and biological factors that interact to create and sustain life on Earth. This includes the air, water, land, atmosphere, plants, animals, and ecosystems. The environment plays a crucial role in providing resources and supporting the complex web of life, including human beings. It is interconnected and dynamic, constantly influenced by natural processes as well as human activities. Environmental protection and conservation are vital to ensure the sustainability and well-being of both present and future generations."
